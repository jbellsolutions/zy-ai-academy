## Channel Mission

Show real parents how AI can transform their child's education -- not with hype or theory, but with actual footage of a 5-year-old doing it every day.

---

## 4 Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Real Sessions (40% of content)
**What:** Actual recordings of Zy working with his AI tutor
**Why:** This is the proof. Nothing sells like watching a 5-year-old happily crush 3rd-grade math.
**Format:** 5-15 minute edited sessions with commentary overlay or intro/outro
**Examples:**
- "Watch My 5yo Do 40 Math Problems (AI Tutoring Session)"
- "Zy's Reading Session - AI Tutor in Action"
- "Real AI Tutoring: What a Daily Session Actually Looks Like"

### Pillar 2: How-To Tutorials (25% of content)
**What:** Step-by-step guides for parents to set up AI tutoring
**Why:** Provides immediate value, ranks for search, builds trust
**Format:** Screen recordings with voiceover, 8-15 minutes
**Examples:**
- "How to Set Up ChatGPT as Your Child's Math Tutor (Free)"
- "The Exact Prompts I Use for My 5yo's AI Tutor"
- "Minecraft Mod Coding for Kids - Complete Setup Guide"

### Pillar 3: Results & Updates (20% of content)
**What:** Progress reports, monthly updates, milestone celebrations
**Why:** Creates ongoing narrative, builds emotional investment
**Format:** Talking head + screen shares, 5-10 minutes
**Examples:**
- "Month 1 Results: What AI Tutoring Did for My Son"
- "From Counting to Multiplication: Zy's 6-Month Progress"
- "My 5yo Just Coded His First Minecraft Mod"

### Pillar 4: Thought Leadership (15% of content)
**What:** Opinions, predictions, responses to AI-in-education news
**Why:** Positions Justin as an authority, attracts shares and discussion
**Format:** Talking head or screen share with commentary, 5-12 minutes
**Examples:**
- "Why Schools Are Getting AI Wrong (A Dad's Perspective)"
- "AI Won't Replace Teachers - Here's What It Actually Does"
- "The Homeschool Advantage in the AI Era"

## Thumbnail Strategy

### Safety First:
- NO close-up photos of Zy's face in thumbnails
- Acceptable: back of head, hands on keyboard, screen recording frames, silhouettes
- Use screen captures of the AI tutor interface with results visible

### Design Guidelines:
- **Brand colors:** Teal (#00BFA6), Orange (#FF6B35), Dark Navy (#1A1A2E), White
- **Font:** Bold sans-serif (Impact, Montserrat Bold, or similar)
- **Text:** 3-5 words maximum on thumbnail, large enough to read on mobile
- **Layout:** 2/3 image, 1/3 text overlay
- **Contrast:** Always use dark text on light or light text on dark -- never low contrast
- **Faces:** Justin's face with expressive reactions works well for thought leadership content

### Thumbnail Templates by Pillar:

**Real Sessions:**
- Screen recording frame showing math problems
- Bold text: "AGE 5" or "40 PROBLEMS" with arrow pointing to screen
- Teal accent color

**How-To Tutorials:**
- Split screen: ChatGPT interface + child-friendly visual
- Bold text: "FREE SETUP" or "STEP BY STEP"
- Orange accent color

**Results/Updates:**
- Before/after layout or progress chart
- Bold text: "MONTH 1 RESULTS" or "IT WORKED"
- Green accent for positive results

**Thought Leadership:**
- Justin talking head with bold text overlay
- Contrarian statement as text: "SCHOOLS ARE WRONG" or "NOT WHAT YOU THINK"
- Dark navy background
