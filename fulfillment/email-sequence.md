## Email 3 -- Day 3

**Subject:** Week 1 tips (from a dad who's been doing this daily)

**From:** Justin from Zy AI Academy

**Body:**

Hey,

You are in your first week. Here are three things I have learned from running this system with my own son every day:

**1. Pick a consistent time.**
Morning works best for most families. Required tutors before lunch, electives after. But whatever works for your schedule -- the key is same time, same place, every day.

**2. Let your child choose electives.**
This is their favorite part. When they pick their own electives, they feel ownership. That is the difference between "I have to do this" and "I want to do this."

**3. Short sessions beat skipped sessions.**
If you only have 15 minutes, do 15 minutes of Math Lab. A half session is infinitely better than no session. Protect the streak.

One more thing -- if you have not set up all 7 tutors yet, block off 15 minutes this weekend to finish. Once they are all installed, daily sessions become effortless.
