## Tier 3 - Online Communities (Organic Engagement Only)

**These are NOT for cold email. Engage organically: share value, answer questions, build relationships.**

### Facebook Groups

| Group Name | Estimated Size | Engagement Level | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| Florida Homeschoolers | 30k+ members | High | General FL homeschool, very active |
| Central Florida Homeschool Community | 10k+ members | High | Orlando metro area focus |
| Orlando Homeschool Network | 5k+ members | Medium | Local meetups and events |
| Tampa Bay Homeschoolers | 10k+ members | High | Tampa area |
| Brevard County Homeschoolers | 5k+ members | Medium | Space Coast area |
| Homeschooling in Florida | 15k+ members | High | Statewide |
| Florida Secular Homeschoolers | 5k+ members | Medium | Secular families |
| Black Homeschoolers of Florida | 3k+ members | Medium | Important diversity reach |
| Florida Homeschool Co-op Finder | 5k+ members | Medium | Families looking for co-ops |
| Tech-Savvy Homeschoolers (National) | 10k+ members | Medium | AI/tech-friendly audience |

### Engagement Strategy for Facebook Groups:
1. Join groups, complete profile (dad who homeschools, AI educator)
2. Spend 2 weeks answering questions and providing value -- no selling
3. Share a "win" post about Zy's progress (organic, not promotional)
4. When asked about methods, mention AI tutoring naturally
5. After building rapport, share a video with context
6. Never post links in first 30 days unless asked directly
