## The Problem

Let's be honest about what's happening in education right now.

**Classrooms are overwhelmed.** The average teacher has 25+ kids and maybe 45 minutes per subject. Your child gets roughly 2 minutes of individual attention per hour. That's not a failure of teachers -- they're doing their best with an impossible setup.

**Tutoring is expensive.** A decent private tutor runs $40-80 per hour. That's $160-320 per week if you want real consistency. Most families can't swing that, and the ones who can are still stuck scheduling around someone else's availability.

**Apps aren't working.** Most "educational" apps are glorified games with a thin layer of learning sprinkled on top. Kids get hooked on the dopamine, not the material. There's no progression, no personalization, no actual teaching happening.

**The gap is growing.** Kids who get extra support at home pull ahead. Kids who don't fall behind. And every year, catching up gets harder.

Here's the thing that keeps me up at night: your kid's potential isn't the problem. The delivery system is the problem.

## The Solution

Zy AI Academy is a **done-for-you AI tutoring system** that runs on your computer using ChatGPT. You don't need to be technical. You don't need to know anything about AI. You just follow the setup guide, and your kid gets a patient, adaptive, endlessly available tutor.

**What makes it different:**

- **It actually teaches.** This isn't a quiz app. The AI tutor explains concepts, walks through problems step by step, adjusts when your kid is confused, and celebrates when they get it right.

- **It's personalized from day one.** You tell the system your child's age, level, and interests. It builds a learning path just for them. No two kids get the same experience.

- **It runs daily without you hovering.** The system comes with daily session templates. Your kid sits down, opens the tutor, and gets to work. You get a summary of what they covered.

- **It makes hard things feel like a game.** The AI adapts its personality and approach to keep your kid engaged. Zy thinks he's having conversations with a friend who happens to know a lot about math.

- **It includes Minecraft mod-coding.** Because nothing motivates a kid like building something cool. Our curriculum teaches real coding concepts through Minecraft modifications -- and it's all guided by the AI tutor.

## How It Works

**Step 1: Set Up (15 minutes)**
Follow our parent setup guide to configure ChatGPT with our custom tutor prompts. It's copy-paste simple. If you can send an email, you can do this.

**Step 2: Start a Session (Daily, 15-30 minutes)**
Your child opens the tutor and begins their daily session. The AI greets them by name, picks up where they left off, and guides them through that day's material.

**Step 3: Watch Them Grow**
Check the progress summaries to see what your child covered, where they excelled, and what they're working on next. That's it. The system does the heavy lifting.

## FAQ

**Do I need to be technical to use this?**
No. If you can copy and paste text and follow a step-by-step guide, you can set this up. The average parent completes setup in about 15 minutes.

**What ages does this work for?**
The system is designed for kids ages 4-12. The AI tutor adjusts its approach, vocabulary, and difficulty based on your child's age and level. Younger kids (4-6) will need a parent nearby for the first few sessions. Older kids (7-12) can work independently.

**Does my child need their own computer?**
They need access to a device with ChatGPT (computer, tablet, or phone). It doesn't need to be their own device -- they can use yours during session time.

**Do I need a ChatGPT subscription?**
The system works best with ChatGPT Plus ($20/month), which gives your child access to the most capable AI tutor. It can work with the free version, but sessions may be limited.

**How much time does this take per day?**
We recommend 15-30 minutes daily. Consistency matters more than duration. Zy does about 20-25 minutes per session.

**What subjects are covered?**
At launch: math and reading. Minecraft coding is included for Founding Family tier and above. We have stretch goals for science and Spanish language tutoring.

**Can I use this for multiple kids?**
Each license covers one family. You can set up different tutor profiles for different children within the same family. If you want to gift it to another family, check out the Family + Friends tier.

**What if it doesn't work for my kid?**
I want this to work for every family. If you're having trouble, reach out to me directly. I'll help you adjust the system for your child's needs. If we genuinely can't make it work, I'll figure out a fair resolution.

**When will I receive the product?**
The core system is already built. Backers will begin receiving access within 2-4 weeks of campaign completion, starting with the earliest tiers.

**Is my child's data safe?**
Your child's sessions happen in your own ChatGPT account. We never see, store, or have access to your child's conversations or data. Everything stays on your account.
