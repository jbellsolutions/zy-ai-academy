## Editing Notes

- **Pacing:** Keep it moving but not rushed. Justin's on-camera segments should feel like a conversation, not a pitch.
- **Music:** Light, upbeat acoustic or piano underscore. Nothing overpowering. Drop music during Zy's real footage to let it feel authentic.
- **Captions:** Add captions throughout. Many parents will watch on mobile without sound.
- **Zy's footage:** The existing three video files are the strongest asset. Use them generously. Real kid + real results is more compelling than any animation.
- **Color grading:** Warm, slightly bright. This is about family, not corporate tech.
- **End card:** Include Kickstarter URL and campaign timeline ("Live now through [date]").
