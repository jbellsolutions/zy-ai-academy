## Pricing Strategy Notes

- **Early Bird at $27** creates urgency and a no-brainer entry point. It's priced below impulse-buy threshold for most parents.
- **Founding Family at $47** is the anchor tier. Most backers will land here. The Minecraft add-on and lifetime updates justify the step up from Early Bird.
- **Limited quantities on every paid tier** drive urgency without feeling fake. The limits are real -- they reflect what Justin can support as a solo founder.
- **VIP and Sponsor tiers** serve dual purpose: high-value backers who need personal attention, and revenue concentration that helps hit stretch goals faster.
- **Post-campaign retail price** will be $97+ for the full system. Every Kickstarter tier represents a genuine discount.
