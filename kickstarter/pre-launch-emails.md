## Email Sequence Technical Notes

**Sending platform:** Use whatever email tool is set up (ConvertKit, Mailchimp, etc.)

**Timing:**
- Email 1: 7 days before launch (Tuesday)
- Email 2: 3 days before launch (Saturday)
- Email 3: Launch day (Tuesday, 8:00 AM EST sharp)

**Segmentation:**
- Send to full notify-me / waitlist
- Tag anyone who opens Email 2 as "high intent" for potential follow-up during campaign week 1

**From name:** Justin from Zy AI Academy
**Reply-to:** Justin's personal email (builds trust, encourages replies)

**A/B testing:** Test subject lines on Emails 1 and 2. Use the winner's pattern for Email 3. If audience is too small to split-test meaningfully, go with the A options -- they're more specific and curiosity-driven.

**Unsubscribe:** Standard footer. Don't fight unsubscribes. Quality list beats big list every time.
