## Emergency Contacts & Backup Plans

**If Kickstarter review is delayed:**
- Submit by April 14 at the latest
- If not approved by April 18, push launch to April 28 (following Tuesday)
- Update email sequence timing accordingly

**If Early Bird sells out Day 1:**
- Celebrate publicly, create urgency for Founding Family tier
- Consider adding 50 more Early Bird spots as a "second wave" (use sparingly)

**If campaign is slow to fund:**
- Double down on personal outreach and community engagement
- Create new content (video, stories) to re-engage interest
- Consider partnering with a parent influencer for mid-campaign push
- Email list with "we need your help" honest appeal

**If technical questions overwhelm support:**
- Create a FAQ document and pin it as a Kickstarter update
- Set up a simple help page with common setup issues
- Prioritize VIP and Founding Sponsor tier questions
- Set clear expectation: 24-48 hour response time
