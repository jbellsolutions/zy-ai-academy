# Zy AI Academy -- Progress Tracker

Print these templates and use them to track your child's learning journey. Consistent tracking helps you spot patterns, celebrate wins, and catch problems early.

## How to Use These Templates

1. **Print what you need.** You do not need to use every template. Start with the Daily Session Log and Weekly Review. Add others as you get into a rhythm.
2. **Keep them visible.** Pin the daily checklist to the wall or clip it to a binder at your child's learning station.
3. **Review together.** The weekly review is most powerful when you do it with your child. Let them see their own progress.
4. **Celebrate milestones.** When they earn a trophy or hit a streak, make a big deal out of it. Recognition fuels motivation.
5. **Adjust as needed.** If a template does not fit your family, modify it. The structure matters more than the format.
