## Community Categories (5 Spaces)

### 1. Wins & Progress
**Description:** Share your child's achievements, milestones, and daily wins. Big or small, we celebrate everything here.
**Post frequency:** Daily (members post their kids' progress)
**Moderation:** Light touch -- encourage, celebrate, highlight best posts weekly

### 2. Setup Help
**Description:** Questions about getting started, tech issues, prompt troubleshooting. No question is too basic.
**Post frequency:** As needed
**Moderation:** Justin or community mods respond within 24 hours to every question
**Rules:** Search before posting, include screenshots when possible

### 3. Minecraft Builds
**Description:** Show off what your kids are building and coding in Minecraft. Share mods, worlds, and creations.
**Post frequency:** 2-3 times/week from active members
**Moderation:** Encourage kids to name their creations and explain what they built

### 4. Feature Requests
**Description:** What do you wish the system could do? What would make your life easier? We're building this together.
**Post frequency:** Weekly
**Moderation:** Justin responds to every request, marks top-voted items as "planned" or "considering"

### 5. Weekly Challenge
**Description:** A new challenge every Monday. Complete it by Friday, earn bonus points. Fun, optional, community-building.
**Post frequency:** 1 challenge post Monday, member responses through the week
**Examples:**
- "Have your child solve 5 word problems and share their favorite one"
- "Ask your child to explain one math concept to YOU using the AI tutor"
- "Build something in Minecraft that represents a math concept"
- "Record a 15-second clip of your child's best moment this week"

## Community Rules & Guidelines

### The Short Version:
Be kind. Be helpful. Celebrate others. No selling.

### Full Guidelines:

**1. Be Supportive**
Every family is at a different stage. Whether someone's child is doing addition or algebra, we celebrate progress at every level. No comparing kids.

**2. No Selling or Self-Promotion**
This is not a marketplace. Don't promote your products, services, courses, or affiliate links. If you have something genuinely useful to share, ask Justin first.

**3. Protect Children's Privacy**
- Never post other people's children's information
- Be thoughtful about what you share about your own children
- No last names of children in posts
- Video/photos of children are at your own discretion, but we encourage caution

**4. Stay On Topic**
This community is about AI-powered education for kids. Political, religious, and off-topic debates belong elsewhere.

**5. Ask Questions Freely**
There are no dumb questions here. If you're stuck, ask. If you're confused, ask. That's what we're here for.

**6. Give Credit**
If you use someone's idea or prompt, give them a shoutout. We're building this together.

**7. Report Issues**
If something feels off -- a post, a member, a technical problem -- DM Justin directly. We'll handle it quickly and privately.

### Violation Policy:
- First offense: Friendly private message
- Second offense: Post removed + formal warning
- Third offense: Removed from community (refund issued if paying member)
- Zero tolerance: Spam, harassment, or child safety violations = immediate removal
